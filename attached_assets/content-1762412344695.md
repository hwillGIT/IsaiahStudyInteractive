### Understanding the Vision Sequence

Group 1: God's Holy Throne Room

Encountering God's throne, seraphim, and holy glory

Group 2: Confession & Cleansing

Recognizing uncleanness and receiving purification

Group 3: Divine Commissioning

Responding to God's call with "Here am I; send me"

Group 4: The Hardening Message

Delivering a message that people won't receive

Group 5: Scope of Judgment

Understanding the extent and duration of discipline

Group 6: Remnant & Holy Seed

Hope for survival through God's faithful remnant
